How we operate

The YAC organizes in subteams, each tackling a subject on project basis. Projects typically last 6 weeks.
  1. Topic. When we start a new project, we choose a subject we want to dive into and give our opinion on. Projects typically last 6 weeks.
  2. Brainstorm one. With a select number of employees, we brainstorm: What is WWF's current opinion? What has been done before? What can be done differently?
  3. Research. We deepdive into the topic, interviewing experts, researching literature. We work out plans to implement new ideas.
  4. Brainstorm two. New ideas and plans are presented and discussed. Potentially, a new round of research is initiated with the feedback.
  5. Delivery. Findings are delivered through a concise advice paper. It is optional to present findings to a larger audience within WWF-NL.
